Privacy Policy • Effective November 29, 2025

NetTrust Nepal does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share share any personal information. Everything happens 100% on your phone — nothing ever leaves your device.

1. No Data Collection

NetTrust Nepal uses on-device machine learning to detect malicious Wi-Fi access points (Evil Twin, Deauthentication attacks, etc.). All scanning, analysis, and results stay on your smartphone. We do not use cloud servers, analytics, or third-party services.

2. Permissions Explained

  • Location permission (Android requirement) – Only used to scan nearby Wi-Fi networks. We do not access your GPS location.
  • Wi-Fi connection information – Required to read network names (SSID), signal strength, and channel data for detection.

No data from these permissions is ever saved or sent anywhere.

3. No Advertising • No Tracking • No Analytics

We do not show ads and do not use Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or any tracking tools.

4. Children’s Privacy

NetTrust Nepal is safe for all ages and complies with COPPA and global privacy standards.
